There is a shortfall of Cybersecurity Talent.

In today's interconnected world, your data is always as risk. Whether its Advanced Persistent Threats or cyber criminal gangs, there is always someone probing your systems, looking for weaknesses, and threatening your ability to conduct business.

To combat this, demand for security practitioners is rising rapidly. However, half of surveyed companies report that they are struggling to find well qualified cybersecurity talent. Only a quarter find cybersecurity program graduates who are well prepared to meet their business needs.

We're here to help fix it.

Inspired by David Brumley's talk How the Best Hackers Learn Their Craft, we have built a curated training pipeline which teaches students to "learn how to learn." Through a mix of Capture the Flag challenges and hands on coding projects, we teach students the fundamentals of computer systems.

Drawing on years of academic, government, and industry experience, Level Up Sec has built a training program covering:

  • Software Development Tools and Best Practices
  • Security Fundamentals
  • Operating System Specifics
  • Red Team Tool Development

By focusing on the the fundamentals, graduates of our program are well prepared to handle the rapidly evolving security landscape.
